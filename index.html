Calibrate AI images. Like a real camera.

Most AI-generated images get color temperature wrong. Models don't truly understand white balance and tend to over-process — leaving images with colors that feel artificial, plastic, or off in ways that are hard to name.

Click the eyedropper on any neutral white point in your image and Film studio auto-balances every channel instantly, the same way a DSLR is calibrated against a grey card. For finer control, adjust Tint and Temperature independently on their own axes.
